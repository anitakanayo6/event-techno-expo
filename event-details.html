About TECHSPO Johannesburg 2023

TECHSPO Johannesburg is a two-day technology expo showcasing the next generation of technology and innovation. The event brings together developers, brands, marketers, technology providers, designers, innovators, and evangelists to set the pace in our advanced world of technology.

  • Date: 2nd - 3rd November 2023
  • Venue: Ticket Pro Dome, Johannesburg
  • Attendees: 4,000+ expected
  • Theme: The Great Reset

CTU Training Solutions at TECHSPO

CTU Training Solutions will have a stand for each of the 2nd-year programs on offer. We are proud to organize the official launch of the exhibition on 2nd November 2023.

The launch event will include:
